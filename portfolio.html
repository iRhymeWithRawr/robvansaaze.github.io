History

﻿With a passion for game creation going back all the way to 2006, I have been active in the independant game & application development community since my days as a child. Starting all the way back at the Yoyogames where my work was featured multiple times, to Gamejolt where I reached the frontpage, to itch.io up until now.

With over 7 years of coding experience and over 15 completely free self published games under my belt (and over 40 non-published ones), I have every tool available to make any game concept or any game mechanic come alive.

Coding and game creation has been my passion for half of my years, and will continue to be for many more.
Positive reviews from end users of several of my games.
